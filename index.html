Alternative Investing

Become a
Private Mortgage Lender

Most investors leave significant returns on the table by staying in traditional markets. Private mortgage lending offers secured, predictable returns backed by real Canadian real estate — without the volatility of stocks or mutual funds.

As a private lender, your capital is secured by a registered mortgage on physical property. You choose the deals, set your terms, and earn consistent monthly income — typically 8–12% annually — while your investment is protected by real assets.

This is conscious wealth creation: your money works intentionally, aligned with tangible value rather than speculation.

🏠
First Mortgage Security

Your investment is registered as a first or second charge on the property. If the borrower defaults, you have legal claim to the asset — the same security a bank holds.

📈
Consistent Passive Income

Receive monthly interest payments directly. Unlike dividends or market returns, private mortgage income is contractually agreed upon before the deal closes.
