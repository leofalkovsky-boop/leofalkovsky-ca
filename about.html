My Story

Why I Became a
Mortgage Broker

I'm Leo Falkovsky — a licensed mortgage broker, CMP designation holder, and licensed real estate agent based in Springwater, Barrie, Ontario. I've lived in this community for over 25 years and I've watched the housing market transform Barrie from a commuter town into one of Ontario's most desirable cities.

I spent years inside the traditional banking system before making the move to independent brokering. That experience opened my eyes to how much money homeowners leave on the table — not from bad intentions, but from a lack of access to strategies the banks simply don't promote.

Manulife One. The Smith Manoeuvre. Rental Cash Damming. Mortgage acceleration. These aren't exotic strategies — they're proven tools that work remarkably well for the right client. My job is to identify when they apply and implement them flawlessly.

Today, I run a client-first practice with one simple promise: my advice is always in your best interest, not the bank's.

I also believe that financial freedom is inseparable from mindset. The most powerful mortgage strategy in the world won't work for someone operating from scarcity. My most successful clients share something beyond good credit — they approach wealth with intention, clarity, and an abundance mindset that transforms how they see every financial decision.
