Alternative Lending

Traditional banks say no every day — to great borrowers. B lenders and credit unions offer flexible qualification using income, equity, and the full picture of your situation.

  • →Designed for clients with bruised credit, recent late payments, or a consumer proposal
  • →Self-employed borrowers who can't prove traditional income — qualified on bank statements or stated income
  • →New Canadians and non-residents with limited Canadian credit history
  • →Higher debt ratios that fall outside conventional bank guidelines
  • →Typically 1–2 year term, then transition to a prime lender once your situation improves
