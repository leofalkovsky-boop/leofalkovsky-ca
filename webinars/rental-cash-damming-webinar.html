The Strategy in Plain English

1
Deposit all rental income directly onto your personal mortgage — it pays down faster.
2
Draw from a HELOC on your personal home to cover rental property expenses (mortgage payments, insurance, repairs, property tax).
3
Because the HELOC was used for an income-producing purpose, its interest is fully tax-deductible under CRA IT-533.
4
Repeat every month. Your non-deductible debt shrinks. Your deductible debt grows. You get bigger tax refunds every year — on the same cash flow.
