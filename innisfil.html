Why Innisfil Is Different

Innisfil's Market Has Unique Financing Challenges

Innisfil has transformed over the past decade. Friday Harbour Resort has added premium waterfront condos and detached homes that require specialist lenders — many mainstream banks decline financing on resort properties or cap loan-to-value ratios lower than expected. In Alcona, new subdivisions along Innisfil Beach Road are popular with young families and Highway 400 commuters looking for more space than Barrie at a lower entry price. And in Lefroy, Gilford, and Belle Ewart, older cottages being converted to year-round residences often need a lender that understands seasonal property transitions.

Friday Harbour & Resort Properties

Resort-zoned properties require specialist lenders. I know which institutions will finance them — and at what terms.
