Why Springwater Is Different

Rural Properties Need Lenders Who Understand Acreage

Springwater Township is home to Midhurst — one of Simcoe County's most sought-after residential areas — as well as smaller communities like Elmvale, Minesing, and Hillsdale where rural and estate properties are common. Rural properties with wells and septic systems require lenders who are comfortable with them. Not every bank will finance a property on a holding tank, or a parcel over 10 acres without a significant down payment premium. I have direct access to lenders who specialize in Springwater's property mix.

Midhurst Executive Homes

Midhurst attracts buyerse professionals and retirees seeking larger lots. Manulife One is particularly effective here given the higher property values and equity positions.

Rural & Acreage Properties

Elmvale, Minesing, and Anten Mills properties often include acreage, wells, and septic — I work with lenders who finance these without imposing high-ratio restrictions.

Estate & Luxury Properties

High-value properties in Vespra and Midhurst benefit from jumbo financing through monoline lenders offering better rates than the major banks.

Smith Manoeuvre for Springwater

Springwater homeowners with significant equity and HELOC access are ideal candidates to convert mortgage interest into CRA-approved investment loan deductions.
