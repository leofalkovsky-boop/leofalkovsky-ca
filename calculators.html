💡 What is an IRD? When you break a fixed mortgage early, lenders charge the greater of: (1) 3 months' interest, or (2) the Interest Rate Differential — the revenue the lender "loses" by re-lending at today's lower rate.

Big banks use posted-rate formulas that dramatically inflate penalties. Monolines use actual market rates — typically a far lower penalty.
